DB Logging Menu

Commands

Turn DB Logging On Turns on logging of information to the database. This information includes voltage and current (from vdcmon) status once per minute, and changes to voltage settings and power as they occur. DB Logging is on by default, and can also be toggled from the command line.
Turn DB Logging Off Turns off logging of information to the database. Information is still logged to local text files in $VHVDIR/DBLogs.
